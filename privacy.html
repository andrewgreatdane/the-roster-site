Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026 · Unhinged Ventures Inc.

The short version: your roster data lives on your device. We don't sell your data, we don't have access to who you're dating, and we never will.

Who We Are

The Roster is developed and published by Unhinged Ventures Inc. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use The Roster app on iOS. Questions? Reach us at privacy@unhingedventures.com.

Data We Do Not Collect

We want to be clear about what we don't do:

  • We do not collect, transmit, or store your roster entries on any server
  • We do not require you to create an account or provide your name or email
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or third parties
  • We do not have access to who you've added to your roster, your notes, ratings, or any personal details you enter into the app

Data Stored On Your Device

All information you add to The Roster is stored locally on your iPhone only — never on our servers. This includes:

  • Roster entries (names, notes, ratings, photos, and other details you enter)
  • Your app PIN, stored securely in your device's encrypted keychain
  • Your premium purchase status and app preferences

If you delete the app, all of this data is permanently removed from your device. We cannot recover it.

Third-Party Services

RevenueCat — used to process and validate in-app purchases. RevenueCat may collect a device identifier and purchase history to manage your subscription status. They do not have access to your roster data. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Expo / EAS — the platform used to build and deliver the app. Expo may collect basic crash reports and diagnostic telemetry to help us identify technical issues. This data does not include your roster entries. See Expo's Privacy Policy.

Apple — as an iOS app distributed through the App Store, Apple's standard platform data practices apply. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

Photos & Camera

The Roster may request access to your camera and photo library so you can add photos to roster entries. Photos you add are stored locally on your device only and are never uploaded to any server. You can revoke these permissions at any time in your iPhone's Settings.

Children's Privacy

The Roster is intended for users 17 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 17. If you believe a child has used the app in violation of this policy, contact us at privacy@unhingedventures.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions or concerns? Reach us at privacy@unhingedventures.com
